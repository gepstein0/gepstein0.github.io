Introduction to Electricity

What I know about electricity:

Power source for:
  • Lights
  • Computers
  • Phones
  • Some cars
Electricity is a form of:
  • Kinetic energy
  • Potential energy
Electrical energy can change forms

Charge:

Two types of charge:
  • Positive
  • Negative
Like charges repel, opposites attract

Atoms are made of:
  • Protons
  • Neutrons
  • Electrons
Charge is conserved (net charge is constant)

Chemical bonds are formed when electrons are:
  • Transferred (ionic)
  • Shared (covalent)
